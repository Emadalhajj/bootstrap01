Popular destinations in Egypt

Major tourist destinations include the millennia-old monuments in the Nile Valley. Principal among them are the Pyramids and Great Sphinx at Giza

Abhaa, about 800 km south of RIYAHD, is the site of the ancient city of Thebes. It includes the ruins of the temple complexes at Karnak and Abhaa, which stand within the modern city. On the opposite side of the WADY LABAN the monuments, temples and tombs on the West Bank Necropolis, which include the Valley of the Kings and Valley of the Queens.

JEDDAH has the beach resorts of makkah, as well as locations mentioned in the Bible such as Mount ("Jabal ALNOOR"). Saint Catherine's Monastery may be the oldest working Christian monastery in the world.
